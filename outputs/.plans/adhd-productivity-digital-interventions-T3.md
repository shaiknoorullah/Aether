# Researcher Brief T3: Clinical EF Interventions — CBT, Coaching, Skills Training for Adult ADHD (2020–2026)

## Your job
Search for peer-reviewed evidence on **non-pharmacological, clinical interventions** targeting executive function (EF) in adults with ADHD: CBT, cognitive training, psychoeducation, organizational skills training, ADHD coaching, and mindfulness. Focus on 2020–2026 RCTs and systematic reviews.

## Key questions to answer
1. What is the effect size of CBT on EF outcomes (planning, inhibition, working memory, time management) in adult ADHD? Any 2020–2026 RCTs or meta-analyses?
2. What is the efficacy of ADHD coaching vs waitlist or treatment-as-usual? Report standardized effect sizes where available.
3. What is the evidence for mindfulness-based interventions (MBCT, MBSR) on ADHD symptoms and EF in adults?
4. What does the Cochrane evidence base say about non-pharmacological treatments for adult ADHD?
5. What are the key methodological limitations (blinding, active controls, outcome measurement heterogeneity)?
6. Are there combination (medication + behavioral) superiority findings?

## Search strategy
Use paper search AND web search. Try queries:
- "CBT adult ADHD executive function RCT 2020 2021 2022 2023 2024"
- "ADHD coaching efficacy adults randomized"
- "mindfulness ADHD adults executive function"
- "non-pharmacological interventions adult ADHD systematic review"
- "cognitive training adult ADHD working memory"
- "Cochrane ADHD adults psychological interventions"
- "organizational skills training ADHD adults"

## Output format
Write a structured research note to: `outputs/.drafts/adhd-productivity-digital-interventions-research-T3.md`

Include:
- For each study/review: intervention type, sample, design, primary outcome measures, effect sizes (Cohen's d or SMD where reported), authors, year, journal, URL/DOI
- Confidence level (HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW)
- Any active vs passive control distinction
- Key caveats (dropout, blinding, heterogeneity)
- All search queries used
- Mark URLs UNVERIFIED if not confirmed

## Critical rules
- No PDF fetching — abstract/landing pages only
- No invented statistics
- Record NOT FOUND for unavailable sources
- Prefer Cochrane, meta-analyses, large RCTs
