# Deep Research Plan: Adult ADHD, Executive Function, Productivity & Digital Interventions (2020–2026)

**Slug:** `adhd-productivity-digital-interventions`  
**Date:** 2026-06-10  
**Scale decision:** Complex multi-domain topic — 4 researcher subagents (economics, clinical outcomes, interventions, digital tools)

---

## Key Questions

1. What is the magnitude of productivity loss and presenteeism attributable to adult ADHD?
2. What are the societal and economic costs (2020–2026), including healthcare, employment, lost GDP?
3. How does adult ADHD affect employment rates, job retention, and earnings relative to controls?
4. What is the measured efficacy of external scaffolding interventions (coaching, CBT, organizational tools) for self-regulation in adults with ADHD?
5. What is the measured efficacy of digital interventions (apps, reminders, gamified tools, wearables, AI-assisted tools) for executive function support in adults?
6. What are the gaps, disagreements, and methodological limitations in this evidence base?
