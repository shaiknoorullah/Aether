# Researcher Brief T1: Browser Market Share 2025–2026

## Objective
Gather current, sourced browser market share statistics. Focus on global data but note regional variation where significant.

## Key Questions
1. What is Chrome's global market share in 2025–2026?
2. What is Safari's share (desktop + mobile combined and separately)?
3. What is Edge, Firefox, Opera, Brave, and Samsung Internet's share?
4. Are there notable regional differences (EU, Asia, mobile-first markets)?
5. Has Chrome's dominance changed over 2023–2026?

## Sources to Target
- StatCounter GlobalStats (gs.statcounter.com)
- SimilarWeb browser stats
- Statista browser market share reports
- Net Marketshare (if still active)
- Any Q1 2026 published data

## Required Output
Write ALL findings to: `outputs/.drafts/browser-market-2026-research-T1.md`

For every statistic include:
- The exact number
- The date range it covers
- The source URL
- The measurement methodology (if known)

Do NOT invent numbers. If a source is paywalled, note the headline from the snippet/preview and mark as "paywalled — partial."
