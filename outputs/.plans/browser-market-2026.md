# Deep Research Plan: Browser Market 2026

**Slug:** `browser-market-2026`
**Date:** 2026-06-10
**Scale decision:** Broad survey / multi-faceted topic → 4 researcher subagents

---

## Key Questions

1. **Market share** – What is the current (2025–2026) global and regional browser market share by browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge, Brave, Opera, Arc, etc.)?
2. **Switching intent** – What do surveys and data say about users' intent to switch browsers, and what drives switching?
3. **AI-browser adoption** – How fast are AI-native browsers (Arc, Brave Leo, Edge Copilot, Opera Aria, Perplexity browser, etc.) being adopted? Are there adoption-rate statistics?
4. **Extension ecosystem** – What is the status of Manifest V2 deprecation vs. V3 adoption? How does MV3 affect ad-blocking and power users? What do current timelines look like?
5. **Competitive dynamics** – What strategic moves are the major browser vendors making in 2025–2026 (partnerships, antitrust rulings, AI integrations, new entrants)?

---

## Evidence Needed

- StatCounter / SimilarWeb / Statista global browser market share data (2025–2026)
- Net Marketshare or other measurement source
- Developer/user surveys (Stack Overflow, browser vendor announcements)
- AI browser adoption numbers (downloads, DAU, press releases)
- Google Chrome MV3 enforcement timeline (official Chrome blog, WICG)
- Firefox / Mozilla position on MV3 and extension support
- DOJ/antitrust rulings affecting Google/Chrome search default deals
- Analyst reports on browser competitive landscape

---

## Scale Decision

**Multi-agent:** The topic covers 5 distinct research domains (market share, switching, AI adoption, extensions, competitive dynamics). Each benefits from dedicated search angles. Using **4 researcher subagents** mapped to domains, then lead synthesis.
