# Researcher Brief T3: Manifest V2 Deprecation, MV3 Limits, Extension Ecosystem

## Objective
Document the current state of Chrome's Manifest V3 rollout, the deprecation of MV2, what MV3 does/doesn't allow, and how this affects the extension ecosystem — especially ad blockers and power users.

## Key Questions
1. What is the current (2025–2026) MV2 deprecation enforcement timeline in Chrome?
2. What specific capabilities does MV3 remove vs. MV2 (webRequest blocking vs. declarativeNetRequest)?
3. How have major extensions (uBlock Origin, AdBlock Plus) responded?
4. What is Firefox's position on MV3 and MV2 support?
5. What is the developer community reaction (extensions removed, ported, workarounds)?
6. Has EU regulation (DMA) or antitrust pressure affected Google's MV3 timeline?
7. What is the current extension ecosystem health metric (number of Chrome Web Store extensions, deprecation notices)?
