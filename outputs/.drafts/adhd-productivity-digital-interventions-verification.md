### 5. MINOR Issues
1. The draft states "EndeavorRx... cleared for pediatric ADHD only (ages 8–12)" — this was based on PubMed search context, not direct FDA confirmation. This is appropriately caveated ("not identified in search").

2. The claim that "no FDA-cleared digital therapeutic exists for adult ADHD" is accurate as of search date but could change. Marked as current-knowledge claim.

3. The 2021 chatbot study (Jang et al.) uses "attention deficit" participants not formally diagnosed with ADHD. This is disclosed in the draft.
